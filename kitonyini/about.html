Welcome to Kitonyini Central SDA Church

Kitonyini Central Seventh-day Adventist Church is a vibrant community of believers committed to following Jesus Christ and sharing His love with our community. We are part of the worldwide Seventh-day Adventist Church family, united in our mission to prepare people for the soon return of Jesus Christ.

The Beginning

Seventh-day Adventist Church - Machakos County, Kenya

The Seventh-day Adventist message reached Kitonyini area through dedicated missionaries and local evangelists who shared the three angels' messages with the community.

Early Years

Church Establishment

A small group of believers began meeting regularly for worship, Bible study, and fellowship. The church was officially organized as Kitonyini Central SDA Church.

Foundation

Growth & Development

Through God's blessing and faithful witnessing, the church grew in membership and influence. Various ministries and departments were established to serve the community.

Expansion

Today

Kitonyini Central SDA Church continues to grow and serve as a beacon of hope in the community, actively involved in evangelism, education, health ministry, and community service.

What We Believe

Seventh-day Adventists accept the Bible as the only source of our beliefs. We consider our movement to be the result of the Protestant conviction Sola Scriptura‚Äîthe Bible as the only standard of faith and practice for Christians.

The Holy Scriptures

The Bible is the inspired Word of God, the foundation of truth.

The Trinity

One God: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, a unity of three co-eternal Persons.

Salvation

Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Sabbath

The seventh day (Saturday) is God's holy day of rest and worship.

Second Coming

Jesus Christ will return personally, visibly, and soon.

Wholistic Health

Our bodies are temples of the Holy Spirit; we practice healthful living.
