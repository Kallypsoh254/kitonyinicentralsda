Our Pastors

Meet the pastoral team serving Kitonyini Central.

Our Pastoral Team

"And I will give you shepherds after my own heart, who will feed you with knowledge and understanding." - Jeremiah 3:15

Senior Pastor

Name: Pastor David Kiprop

Education: Master of Divinity, Andrews University

Experience: 15+ years in pastoral ministry

Pastor David leads our congregation with a passion for expository preaching and community outreach. He oversees the overall spiritual direction of the church and provides pastoral care to members.

Associate Pastor

Name: Pastor Sarah Wanjiku

Education: Bachelor of Theology, University of Eastern Africa

Experience: 8 years in youth and family ministry

Pastor Sarah specializes in youth ministry and family life education. She coordinates Sabbath School programs and leads our young adult fellowship.

Youth Pastor

Name: Pastor Michael Oduya

Education: Bachelor of Arts in Religious Studies

Experience: 5 years in youth evangelism

Pastor Michael works with our youth department, organizing camps, retreats, and community service projects. He is passionate about mentoring the next generation of church leaders.

Children's Ministry Coordinator

Name: Pastor Grace Ndung'u

Education: Early Childhood Education Diploma

Experience: 10 years in children's ministry

Pastor Grace oversees our children's Sabbath School and junior youth programs. She creates engaging, age-appropriate spiritual education for our youngest members.
